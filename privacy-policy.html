Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Nova Workout And Gym Tracker (the "App") is operated by NOVASTORE (PTY) LTD ("we", "us", "our") and how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the App.

We are based in South Africa and aim to comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

Who we are

Operator (Responsible Party): NOVASTORE (PTY) LTD
App name: Nova Workout And Gym Tracker
Contact email: support@novastore.co.za

What this policy covers

This policy applies to personal information processed through the App, including account information, fitness and progress data you create in the App, purchase/subscription status, advertising-related data where applicable, and limited technical data needed to keep the App working.

Information we collect and store

We collect only what is needed to provide the features you use.

Account and profile information

If you sign in or create a profile, we may store:

  • Email address
  • Display name or nickname
  • Unique user identifier (UID)
  • Photo URL, such as an avatar image link you choose
  • Search key, such as a lowercase version of your name for search

Fitness and app data you create

We may store data you enter or generate while using the App, such as:

  • Activity level and fitness goal
  • Daily step goal and weekly goal days
  • Workout and activity tracking data, such as dates, routines, exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration, and notes
  • Streak and progress values, including current streak, best streak, and streak freezes
  • Quest-related data, such as daily quests, generated dates, rerolls, and completions
  • App preferences and cosmetic selections, including themes, borders, and unlocked items
  • In-app currency balances, totals, and other progression stats
  • Friends feature data, if you use it, such as friend identifiers and related counters

Subscriptions and in-app purchases

The App offers an optional monthly subscription to remove ads and optional purchases of in-app currency.

We may store:

  • Subscription entitlement status, such as adsRemoved or isProMember
  • In-app currency balances and related totals
  • Purchase verification metadata needed to confirm entitlements

Payments are processed by Google Play and/or the Apple App Store. We do not store your full payment card details.

Advertising data (Google AdMob)

If you do not subscribe to remove ads, the App may display ads using Google AdMob (Google).

AdMob and its partners may collect and process information such as:

  • Device identifiers, such as the advertising ID
  • IP address and general network information
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data, including impressions, clicks, and frequency

You can control ad personalization and reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings and Google account settings.

Limited technical and diagnostic data

To keep the App reliable and secure, we may collect limited technical information such as:

  • App version, device or OS version, and language
  • Basic logs and crash or error information

How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the App features, including workouts, goals, streaks, quests, friends, and preferences
  • Save and sync your data where applicable
  • Deliver in-app currency and cosmetic unlocks
  • Process and verify subscriptions and purchases
  • Show ads to non-subscribed users and measure ad performance
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

Where and how your data is processed

We use reputable third-party service providers to help operate the App, including Google Firebase for authentication and cloud data storage and processing.

These providers may process data on servers located outside South Africa. Where cross-border processing occurs, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information for money.

We may share information:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App
  • With advertising partners (Google AdMob) for non-subscribed users
  • With app stores and payment providers to process purchases
  • If required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • As part of a business transaction, such as a merger or acquisition, subject to safeguards

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy.

  • Account and workout data is kept until you delete your account or request deletion
  • Purchase records may be retained for accounting, compliance, and support
  • Logs and diagnostics are generally retained for limited periods

Your rights and choices

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing where applicable
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise your rights, email support@novastore.co.za.

Permissions

The App may request optional permissions, depending on features you use, such as storage or photos if you choose to upload images or attachments, and notifications for reminders if enabled.

You can manage permissions in your device settings.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational security measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support@novastore.co.za and we will take steps to delete it.

Third-party services

The App may use third-party services or SDKs including Google Firebase, Google AdMob, Google Play Billing, and Apple In-App Purchase. These third parties may collect and process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide notice in the App.
