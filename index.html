Built around consistency

Nova is not just a basic logbook. It is designed to make progress visible, rewarding, and easier to maintain with a clean system of plans, quests, streaks, stats, and social motivation.

01

Workout planning

Create structured sessions, repeat routines through the week, and keep your training schedule organized in one place.

02

Daily quests

Complete smaller objectives like mobility, hydration, and logging activity to keep momentum alive every day.

03

Streak and XP system

Turn consistency into progression with visible streaks, Nova XP, rank movement, and unlockable rewards.

04

Social ranking

Compete with friends, compare progress, and make your fitness journey feel connected instead of isolated.

05

Profile identity

Customize your look with titles, borders, avatars, and cosmetics earned through activity and progression.

06

Lifetime stats

Review long-term milestones, best streaks, total workouts, and activity records as your account grows.
